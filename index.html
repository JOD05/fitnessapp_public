Privacy Policy

Ascent uses an account so your training data follows you across devices. Your data is stored securely in the cloud, tied to your account, and is never sold or used for advertising. Two optional features send data to an AI service to give you estimates. The details are below.

Your account

To use Ascent you create an account using your email address and a password, or with Sign in with Apple. We use this only to authenticate you and to associate your data with you. If you use Sign in with Apple, Apple may provide a private relay email rather than your real address.

What the app stores

When you use Ascent, the following information is stored in your account: your profile (name, and the height, age, and sex used for energy estimates), your goals and targets, your supplement list, your generated or custom workout programs, your logged workouts and sets, bodyweight and body-fat entries, and nutrition entries. A copy is also cached on your device so the app works offline; the cloud copy is the source of truth across your devices.

Where your data is stored

Your account and data are stored using Supabase, a cloud database and authentication provider, on infrastructure they operate. Access is restricted by per-user security rules so that each account can only access its own data. Supabase processes this data on our behalf; see Supabase's privacy policy.

AI estimate features (optional)

Two features send data off your device to produce an estimate. They run only when you choose to use them:

  • Meal photo estimate. If you take or select a meal photo, the image is sent through our processing endpoint (hosted on Cloudflare) to Anthropic's API, which returns an approximate calorie and protein range. The photo is processed to produce the estimate and is not stored by our endpoint.
  • AI program setup. If you use the "Describe what you want" / "Set up with AI" option, the text you enter is sent through the same endpoint to Anthropic to suggest training settings. It is used to generate suggestions and is not stored by our endpoint.

Anthropic processes this data to return a result and, per its commercial terms, does not use API inputs or outputs to train its models. See Anthropic's privacy policy. If you never use these features, no images or goal text leave your device for AI processing.

Barcode lookups (optional)

If you scan a product barcode, the barcode is sent to Open Food Facts, an open food database, to retrieve nutrition information. No account or personal data is included in that lookup. See Open Food Facts' privacy policy.

What we don't do

We do not sell your data. The app contains no advertising and no third-party tracking or analytics. We do not share your data except with the service providers above that are necessary to run the app (cloud storage, the AI estimate features, and barcode lookups), each acting on our behalf or on your explicit action.

Retention and deleting your data

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete account), which removes your account and the data associated with it from the cloud database. You can also clear the on-device copy by deleting the app. To request deletion or ask a question, email jamesdemoya@gmail.com.

Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored with per-user access controls. No system is perfectly secure, but we use these standard protections and store only what the app needs to function.
